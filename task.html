Chicken salad recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Cooked chicken breasts 4 cups
Lettuce One pack
Tomatoes A few slices
Cucumber A few slices
Salad sauce 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Wash all the vegetables
  2. Cook your chicken
  3. Cut tomatoes and cucumber into smaller pieces
  4. Chop the lettuce into smaller bits
  5. Place the cutten vegetables in a bowl
  6. Add chicken
  7. Add sauce on top and enjoy!
